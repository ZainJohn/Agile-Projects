## Goals and Motivations

- Purchase the latest tech products quickly and conveniently.
- Compare detailed specs and reviews before committing to a purchase.
- Track orders in real time and receive timely delivery notifications.
- Discover new products through personalised recommendations.

---

## Challenges and Obstacles

- Finding a trustworthy site that stocks cutting-edge tech products.
- Inconsistent product descriptions make comparison difficult.
- Slow or complicated checkouts lead to cart abandonment.
- Limited payment options – prefers digital wallets and buy-now-pay-later.

---

## Frustrations

- Poor mobile experience – website not optimised for smartphones.
- Out-of-stock items with no restock notification feature.
- Lack of detailed technical specifications on product pages.
- Hidden fees revealed only at checkout.
